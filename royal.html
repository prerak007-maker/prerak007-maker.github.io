Royal Enfield Himalayan

The current model is powered by a 411cc, single-cylinder, air and oil-cooled, fuel-injected engine, which churns a maximum power of 24.83PS @ 6500rpm and a maximum torque of 32Nm @ 4250rpm mated to a 5-speed gearbox. The total fuel tank capacity of the bike is 15 litres. The Himalayan is fitted with 21-inch front and 18-inch rear tube-type radial tyres. The suspension setup consists of telescopic forks with 200mm of wheel travel at the front and a rear monoshock, which offers 180mm wheel travel.

Royal Enfield Continental GT 650

The Continental GT 650 gets clip-on handlebars, a single seat with a race cowl, a smaller 12.5-litre fuel tank and black spoked wheels. The speedometer is a twin-pod analogue unit with a small digital display for the odometer and trip meters. The Continental GT also makes do with conventional bulbs in its headlight, taillight and indicator. It's offered in five colour options - Black Magic (metallic black), Ventura Blue (metallic blue), Mister Clean (chrome), Dr Mayhem (black and grey) and Ice Queen (metallic white). Powering the bike is a BS4-compliant 648cc, air and oil-cooled, 270-degree parallel-twin motor that makes 47.6PS of power at 7100rpm and a peak torque of 52Nm at 5200rpm.

Royal Enfield Thunderbird 500X

The Thunderbird 500X is the newest addition to the Thunderbird family.
